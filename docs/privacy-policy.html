Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to myAIcoach ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

myAIcoach is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Conversation Data: Your conversations with AI coaches are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your conversations.
  • Custom Coaches: Any custom coaches you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to our premium service, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status, not your payment details.
  • API Usage: When you chat with a coach, your messages are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. These messages are subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the coaching service
  • To process your subscription through Google Play
  • To generate AI responses to your coaching conversations

4. Data Storage

All your personal data (conversations, custom coaches, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal information. If you uninstall the app, all local data is deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: For generating AI coaching responses. Messages you send are processed by OpenAI.
  • Google Play Billing: For processing premium subscriptions.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscription status.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. Since most data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data also depends on the security of your device.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Delete individual conversations or coaches within the app
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
